History

Founded in 1990 by outdoor enthusiasts Jake and Sarah Larson, RiverQuest Rafting Adventures began with a single raft on the Colorado River. The couple aimed to share their passion for white-water rafting while prioritizing safety and environmental stewardship.

As outdoor tourism grew in the early 2000s, RiverQuest expanded its offerings to include various guided trips, catering to both beginners and seasoned adventurers. By 2010, the company became known for its commitment to sustainable tourism, implementing eco-friendly practices like solar-powered camps and river cleanup efforts. Today, RiverQuest welcomes thousands of guests each year, delivering thrilling rafting experiences while respecting nature.

What you will find at EE Rafting Club

At Extreme Expedition Rafting Club, we offer a range of exhilarating white water rafting trips designed to suit all levels of adventure seekers. Whether you're looking for a thrilling ride or a leisurely float, we've got the perfect experience for you!



~Beginner Adventures~
Perfect for families and first-timers, our beginner trips take you through gentle waters where you can learn the basics of rafting while enjoying stunning scenery. Our friendly guides will teach you essential paddling techniques, ensuring you feel comfortable and confident as you navigate the river's twists and turns.



~Intermediate Thrills~
Ready to up the ante? Our intermediate trips are designed for those with some rafting experience. Tackle exciting Class II and III rapids that will get your adrenaline pumping, all while surrounded by breathtaking landscapes. This is the ideal way to challenge yourself and build your skills!



~Expert Expeditions~
For seasoned rafters seeking the ultimate thrill, our expert trips take you on a wild ride through Class IV and V rapids. Brace yourself for heart-stopping drops, powerful currents, and unforgettable moments that will leave you craving more. These adventures are not for the faint of heart, but for those who live for the rush of the river!



~Scenic Floats~
If you prefer a more relaxed experience, our scenic floats offer a peaceful journey down calm waters. Enjoy the beauty of nature as you paddle at your own pace, perfect for families and anyone looking to unwind while soaking in the stunning views.



~Custom Group Trips~
Planning a group adventure? We can customize a trip tailored to your group's interests and skill levels. Whether it's a corporate retreat, family reunion, or a day out with friends, we'll create a memorable experience that everyone will cherish.



Join us at Extreme Expedition Rafting Club and let the river be your guide! Each trip promises adventure, laughter, and memories that will last a lifetime. Book your adventure today and get ready to make a splash!
